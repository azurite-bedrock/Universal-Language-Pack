# Trademark Policy

This document describes how the names, logos, and brand identity associated with **Azurite** and the **Universal Language Pack** (collectively, "the Project") may be used.

This policy is separate from, and additional to, the licenses governing the Project's source code and content. Open-source licenses do not grant trademark rights.

## Marks Covered

This policy covers:

- The name **"Azurite"** and any associated logos, wordmarks, or stylizations
- The name **"Universal Language Pack"** and abbreviations such as **"ULP"**
- Any project-specific iconography distributed with the Project

These marks are owned by the Azurite community and are protected as common-law trademarks in jurisdictions where such protection applies, regardless of whether they are formally registered.

## Permitted Uses (No Permission Needed)

You may use the Project's marks, without prior permission, to:

- Refer to the Project in articles, blog posts, tutorials, videos, streams, and other commentary
- Link to the Project's official repositories, websites, and downloads
- Indicate that your modpack, server, or resource pack uses or is compatible with the Project, provided you do not imply endorsement
- Reproduce the marks unmodified for the purpose of accurate factual reference

When using the marks under this section, please:

- Use the marks in their unmodified form
- Make clear that you are not the Project or its maintainers
- Avoid any presentation that suggests official sponsorship or affiliation

## Uses Requiring Permission

You must obtain written permission from the Azurite community before:

- Using the Project's marks in the name, logo, or branding of a product, service, paid offering, or commercial venture
- Using the Project's marks in a way that could reasonably be confused with official Project communications or releases
- Distributing modified forks of the Project under the same name or a confusingly similar name
- Registering domain names, social media handles, or trademark applications that incorporate the Project's marks

To request permission, open an issue on the Project's GitHub repository or contact the maintainers directly through the channels listed in the README.

## Forks

The Project's source code and content are licensed separately (see `README.md` and the `LICENSES/` directory). Those licenses **do not** grant trademark rights.
If you fork the Project without intention of contributing to the original:

- You may distribute your fork under the applicable code and content licenses
- You need to rename your fork. Do not use "Azurite," "Universal Language Pack," or "ULP" in the name of your fork or its distribution channels
- You must state factually that your fork is "based on" or "derived from" the Project, provided this does not imply endorsement

## Reporting Misuse

If you become aware of a use of the Project's marks that appears to violate this policy, please open an issue on the Project's GitHub repository or contact the maintainers directly.

This policy may be updated from time to time. The current version applies to all uses from its publication date forward.
